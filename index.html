Feature Proposal

Internal request document — Automated VAST Tag Validation & Data Safety Checker.

Feature Name

Automated VAST Tag Validation & Data Safety Checker

Problem Statement

VAST tag validation today is heavily manual and dependent on individual technical expertise. In a cross-device setup, this creates a significant bottleneck for AMs and Ad-Ops as scale increases.

  • Non-compliant VAST tags are occasionally approved despite failing internal checks
  • Real risk of unauthorized data sharing with non-approved third-party vendors
  • Ad rendering failures and broken client-side measurement
  • Cross-device validations consume disproportionate AM time and slow launches

Proposed Feature

1
Hard-enforce VAST 2.0 — any other version is immediately rejected
2
Domain allowlist — any unknown domain in the tag is flagged as a violation
3
Wrapper detection — validate URI encoding, trace inner tag, check 1080×1920 creative compliance
4
Bulk validation — upload a sheet with hundreds of tags and get a full compliance report in seconds

Impact

🚀
Near-Zero TAT
Manual validation eliminated. Cross-device approvals near-instant.
⏱
AM Time Savings
Hours of rechecking removed. Capacity freed for strategy.
❌
Data Risk Reduced
No accidental approval of unapproved vendor pixels.
💰
Revenue Impact
Faster go-lives, less rework, scale without headcount.
